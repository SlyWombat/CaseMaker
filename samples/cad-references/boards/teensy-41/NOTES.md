# Teensy 4.1 — CAD references

PJRC has historically NOT released KiCad / Eagle PCB source for Teensy 4.1; the layout was done in Pads. The schematic and dimensions are public.

## Bundled here

- `teensy41_schematic.png` — full schematic (PNG export from PJRC).
- `teensy41_dimensions.html` — PJRC dimensions HTML (PCB outline + mounting holes).

## Canonical sources

- Product page: https://www.pjrc.com/store/teensy41.html
- Schematic: https://www.pjrc.com/teensy/schematic41.png
- Dimensions: https://www.pjrc.com/teensy/dimensions.html
- Pinout cards (front / back): https://www.pjrc.com/teensy/pinout.html

## Community KiCad

- jenschr/Teensy-4.1-example — community KiCad files reproducing the official schematic. Useful as a cross-check, but read pin-by-pin against the official PNG before trusting any single placement.

## License

PJRC publishes the schematic and dimensions for public reference; treat their assets as PJRC-owned and link rather than redistribute the high-resolution sources. The PNG bundled here is small and used for our own engineering validation.
