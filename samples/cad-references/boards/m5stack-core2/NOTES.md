# M5Stack Core2 — CAD references

## Bundled here

- `CORE2_V1.0_SCH.pdf` — official M5Stack schematic for the Core2.

## Canonical sources

- Product page: https://docs.m5stack.com/en/core/core2
- Schematic: https://m5stack-doc.oss-cn-shenzhen.aliyuncs.com/644/CORE2_V1.0_SCH.pdf
- M5Stack Arduino library (no CAD, just firmware): https://github.com/m5stack/M5Core2

## Notes

M5Stack does not publish KiCad / Allegro source for the Core2. The schematic above + the docs.m5stack.com mechanical photos are the best available reference. PCB outline + mounting holes for the M5 stackable form factor are stable across the line and documented at https://docs.m5stack.com/en/core/core2.
