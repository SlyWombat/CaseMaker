# Raspberry Pi Pico — CAD references

## Bundled here

- `pico-datasheet.pdf` — full datasheet including mechanical drawing.
- `RPI-PICO-R3-PUBLIC-SCHEMATIC.pdf` — official schematic (Rev 3).
- `RPI-PICO-R3-PUBLIC.DSN` — Cadence OrCAD design file.
- `RPI-PICO-R3a-PUBLIC.brd` — Cadence Allegro PCB file.
- `RPI-PICO_PCB-R3_BOM-R3-PUBLIC.xlsx` — bill of materials.
- `LICENSE.txt` — upstream license terms.

## Canonical sources

- Datasheet: https://datasheets.raspberrypi.com/pico/pico-datasheet.pdf
- Design files (zip): https://datasheets.raspberrypi.com/pico/RPi-Pico-R3-PUBLIC-20200119.zip
- Documentation: https://www.raspberrypi.com/documentation/microcontrollers/pico-series.html
